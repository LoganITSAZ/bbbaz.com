Meet Catrina Popelier RN IBCLC

Registered nurse and board-certified lactation consultant, dedicated to supporting & empowering Arizona families through every stage of their breastfeeding journey.

Credentials

  • Registered Nurse (RN)
  • International Board Certified Lactation Consultant (IBCLC)
  • Evidence-based, trauma-informed, infant-centered support

I Specialize In

  • Breastfeeding and pumping
  • Postpartum mental health and infant mental health
  • Infant & toddler development
  • Pregnancy and birth
  • Transitioning NICU babies home

Common Topics I Help With

  • Preparing for breastfeeding: reading, videos, pump selection
  • Milk supply and feeding plans: on-demand vs scheduled, return-to-work
  • Latch, positioning, and nipple shields (use and weaning)
  • Comfort and healing: sore nipples, clogged ducts, mastitis
  • Oral ties and feeding assessments (with referrals as needed)
  • Growth, sleep, and soothing: weight gain, night feeds, gas/colic
